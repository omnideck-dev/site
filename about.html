About Omnideck

Omnideck is an open-source AI agent workbench you run on your own machine — not someone else's cloud. You own your agents, your models, your machine.

Why Omnideck?

Cloud AI agents are convenient until they aren't. Your conversations, files, and credentials flow through servers you don't control. You're locked to one model per tool. Autonomous background tasks are expensive add-ons or simply missing.

Omnideck is freedom-first: everything runs in a container on your hardware, you bring any LLM you want, and all your data stays on your machine.

What it does

  • Multi-agent workbench — build a team of specialized agents, each with its own model, prompt, and tool access
  • Local-first — everything runs in a container on your machine; no connections to Omnideck servers
  • Model-agnostic — connect any LLM: OpenAI, Anthropic, Ollama, OpenRouter, or any OpenAI-compatible endpoint
  • Autonomous tasks — schedule goals that run in the background and notify you on completion
  • Integrated tools — browser automation, code execution, filesystem access, persistent memory, and email integrations (Gmail and iCloud) out of the box
